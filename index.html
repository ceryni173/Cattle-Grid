A Simple Grid

Initially, this is all that was meant to come of this. I wanted a grid that I could use whenever, with no js library attachments, no bloat and no extra addons that are just unnecessary. If you want just the grid, grab _grid.scss from assets/scss/general and import it into your stylesheets!
